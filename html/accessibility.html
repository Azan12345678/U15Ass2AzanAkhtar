Our Commitment

VR World is committed to making our website accessible to all users, including people with disabilities.

Accessibility Features

  • Clear and simple navigation.
  • High contrast text and backgrounds.
  • Keyboard-friendly controls.
  • Alternative text for images.
  • Readable fonts and layouts.

Using the Website

All main pages can be accessed using a keyboard. Images include descriptive text to support screen readers.
