Accessibility at VR PULSE

At VR PULSE, we believe virtual reality should be accessible to everyone. Our facilities, equipment and services are designed to provide inclusive experiences for guests with a wide range of needs. We continually improve our environment to ensure every visitor can enjoy immersive technology comfortably and confidently.

Wheelchair Accessibility

Spacious play areas and accessible pathways allow easy movement throughout the venue.

Seated VR Experiences

Many experiences can be enjoyed while seated, offering flexibility and comfort for all participants.

Staff Assistance

Our trained team is available to provide setup support, guidance and personalised assistance.

Adjustable Controls

VR systems can be configured to suit individual preferences and accessibility requirements.

Inclusive Experiences

A selection of family-friendly, educational and low-intensity experiences are available.

Need Additional Support?

If you have specific accessibility requirements, please contact us before your visit. Our team will work with you to ensure the best possible VR experience and make any reasonable accommodations available.
